SDE User Registration Guide

A short user guide on how to self-register (create a new user) from the registration link

## Part 1

In your `email inbox` a message from your company Admin will contain the invitation link,

* click on the `Registration Form` link next to the attachment section

**Figure 1: example of the attached invitation link**

<img src="https://github.com/orensrauch/The_Software_Testing_Router/blob/main/registration/1.png?raw=true" alt="1" width="70%"/>

>Note: you may have advanced security policy that require OTP verification code, the OTP code will be sent to you via mobile phone or additional email message.


## Part 2

Complete your user details then click on `Submit` to finish registration process

**Figure 2: Registration form for the sign-up process**

<img src="https://github.com/orensrauch/The_Software_Testing_Router/blob/main/registration/2.png?raw=true" alt="2" width="70%"/>
